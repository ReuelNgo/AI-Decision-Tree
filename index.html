About this tool

The AI Agent Platform Advisor is an internal decision-support tool built by Bangkok Bank to help teams identify the most suitable Microsoft AI platform for their agent use case.

What it does

It guides you through a short, structured questionnaire covering your goal, target users, data sources, deployment channels, customisation needs, and governance requirements. Based on your answers it recommends one of four Microsoft platforms:

  • Microsoft 365 Copilot — AI assistance inside the apps your team already uses
  • Agent Builder — Declarative, no-code agents that extend M365 Copilot
  • Copilot Studio — Low-code conversational agents and process automation
  • Microsoft Foundry — Pro-code, fully custom AI development

Data & privacy

This tool runs entirely in your browser. No answers or results are transmitted to any server.

Based on

Microsoft's official Cloud Adoption Framework · AI Strategy decision tree.

Scoring Methodology

Each question in the advisor maps to one or more platform dimensions. Your answer awards weighted points to each of the four platforms. The platform with the highest total score at the end is recommended as your primary choice.

Dimensions scored

DimensionDescription
Goal alignmentHow well the platform fits the stated business objective
User profileWhether the intended users are knowledge workers, makers, or developers
Data sourcesCompatibility with where your data lives (M365 Graph, enterprise systems, custom APIs)
ChannelsSupport for the deployment channels required (Teams, web, mobile, custom)
Customisation depthDegree of logic, branding, or integration complexity needed
GovernanceCompliance, audit, and IT control requirements

Confidence rating

A confidence percentage is shown alongside the recommendation. This reflects the margin between the top-scoring platform and the next closest. A tight margin surfaces an "Also consider" suggestion.

Limitations

The advisor is a starting point, not a final decision. Edge cases, existing licensing agreements, and organisational constraints may shift the recommendation. Always validate with your IT and governance teams.
